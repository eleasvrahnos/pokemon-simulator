Welcome to the Pokémon Simulator, !

You will start with a single random Pokémon and go through a number of phases. This cycle of phases will happen a total of 5 times. Complete all 5 cycles and you win the game!

Phase 1: CATCHING

3 Pokémon with randomized moves will appear, and you will have 1 chance to catch each. If successful, the Pokémon is added to your party. If your party is already full of 6 Pokémon, you will have the opportunity to release a Pokémon from your party. These Pokémon will fight alongside you in future gym battles.

Phase 2: GYM BATTLES

You will go against three Pokémon trainers of a chosen gym type, each with Pokémon matching the type of the gym you are fighting in. Defeat 2 of the 3 gym trainers to get the gym badge. If you fail, it's game over!
